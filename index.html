About Us

Do you love gardening but dislike the time,effort and cost of taking the refuse to the dump?

We have the fast, easy and cost effective solution, with a simple subscription to efficiently get rid of your garden waste. Our collection service takes away your leaves, branches and organic garden matter weekly saving you the hasle for as little as R69/month.

While anyone can still take their own garden waste to to the landfill or dumap or put it in the green bags for collection, but we know how much waste our Durban gardens produce. So do the smart thing and get Drum On The Go to do the "dirty work" for you.
